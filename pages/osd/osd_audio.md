Adjust audio configurations.

### `Volume`

Set the volume level of headphone output. 

#### On Entering `Volume`

Use the joystick to adjust the volume level from 0 to 100.

### `Reset Audio`

Reset your monitor's `Audio` settings to factory defaults.
